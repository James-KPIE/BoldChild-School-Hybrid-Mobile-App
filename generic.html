About Us

Motto: In God We Trust, so We Put our Hope in HIM.

History

The School opened her door fr business on the 1st of February, 2010 at 7:00am in the mourning

The name the school started with was "THE ANTHONIAN SCHOOL HOUSE"; and she opened with the DAYCARE section with infants and toddlers from ages 3months - 2years.

Mission Statement

  1. To raise children with excellent knowledge and understanding of the time.
  2. To build self-confidence through sound education for tomorrow's leaders
  3. To develop in the child, right attitude to proper learning and acquiring new knowledge.
  4. To inculcate good and moral behaviours through effective teaching/learning process.

More About Our School...

BoldChild School has one big library where amazing books are kept which are very rich in knowledge. Facilities like head office, clerk office, science laboratory, computer lab having very new computers, staff room, one big sports ground etc. Our school has good qualified trained and experienced teachers who teach in very effective and creative manner so pupils can can excel with their studies. The school have brilliant pupils who always rank higher in the competitions held outside the school or inside the school. We all go to school in the proper uniform. We have two types of the uniform, one common uniform and other white uniform mainly for physical training class, and a custom made native attire as a Friday wear. All Pupils assemble in the playground in the morning for Morning Prayer and then go to their respective class rooms. BoldChild school organizes an annual function for all classes every year which we must take part. Every Child loves BoldChild School.
